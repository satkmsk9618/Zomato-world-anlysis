The dashboard is split into two primary pages, offering a complete picture from a macro (World) to a micro (Performance) level.

1. World Analysis
This page focuses on the geographical spread and macro-level data.

Global Footprint Map: Visualizes restaurant density and location across continents including Europe , Asia , Africa , North America (NAM) , South America (SAM) , and Oceania/Australia.

High-Value Restaurants: Displays the Top 5 Restaurants by Average Cost, including examples like Sushi Masa and Satoo Hotel Shangri-La.

Key Filters: Allows users to segment data by Country , City , Rating Color , Has Table booking , and Has Online delivery.

2. Performance Analysis
This page dives into brand performance and specific market details.

Brand Dominance Treemap: Highlights the most prominent restaurant chains (likely by count or votes), such as Cafe Coffee Day , Domino's Pizza , Subway , McDonald's , Pizza Hut , and Baskin Robbins.

Currency and Cost Context: The analysis on this page is contextualized using the Botswana Pula (P) currency, with a default Price Range filter of 17K.

Location Spotlight: Pinpoints a specific location analyzed, such as Bakers & More, Asian Institute of Medical Sciences, Badkal Flyover, Sector 21 A, Sector 21, Faridabad.
